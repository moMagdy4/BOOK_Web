About

Libraries are cornerstones of our communities as hubs for knowledge, research, history, and so much more ,
and to make accessing books easier, we decided to make FCIS Library.
FCIS Library is an open library catalog building towards a web page for every book everyone needs.
Our long term goal is to capture all books in digital form.
